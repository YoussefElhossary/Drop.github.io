About Us
Drop is an Egyptian startup founded in 2022, with the aim to provide digital and easy to use automotive solutions of quality.

Redefining the entire car services industry, starting by developing our very own on-demand, steam-based car wash. While connecting car owners to certified workshops in their city to service, repair or customized their cars.
